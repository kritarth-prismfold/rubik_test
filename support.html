FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we receive most often about our services, support, and approach.

What industries does Rubik Infotech serve?

Rubik Infotech works with mid-market and enterprise organisations across manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, BFSI (banking, financial services and insurance), KPO/BPO services, engineering, and professional services. Our security frameworks are adapted to the specific compliance and operational requirements of each sector — including Schedule M for pharma, RBI guidelines for financial services, and ISO 27001 across all industries.

How does Rubik Infotech approach a new security engagement?

Every engagement begins with an architecture and risk assessment phase. We spend time understanding your environment — your existing technology stack, your threat exposure, your regulatory obligations, and your operational constraints. We do not propose solutions before we understand the problem. This approach ensures that our recommendations are proportionate, practical, and designed for your specific context rather than a generic template.

Do you work with organisations that already have existing security tools in place?

Yes — the majority of our engagements involve organisations that already have security tools deployed. Our initial assessment typically includes evaluating what you have, identifying gaps or misconfiguration, and recommending whether to optimise existing tools or supplement them. We are vendor-agnostic and will work with your existing investments where possible rather than recommending a complete replacement.

What is the typical timeframe for a security architecture review?

A standard security architecture review typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on the size and complexity of your environment. This covers a structured assessment of your network, cloud, endpoint, and identity controls against a defined framework, followed by a written report with prioritised recommendations and a proposed roadmap. Larger or more complex environments — such as multi-site operations or OT-integrated networks — may require longer assessment periods.

Can Rubik Infotech manage our software licences on an ongoing basis?

Yes — our licensing practice provides full lifecycle management. This includes initial procurement and activation, ongoing utilisation monitoring, renewal management with advance notice, compliance reporting, and optimisation recommendations. We provide monthly utilisation reports and flag upcoming renewals at 90, 60, and 30 days in advance. Our licence management service covers all major platforms including Microsoft, Google Workspace, Adobe, Zoom, AutoCAD, and others.

What support is available for emergency security incidents?

Organisations on our Professional and Enterprise support plans have access to incident response support. Our Enterprise plan includes a 24×7 emergency hotline and a 1-hour response SLA for critical incidents. For organisations not on a support plan, we offer ad-hoc incident response services — contact us directly at +91 9909033295 or contact@rubikinfotech.com. We strongly recommend having an incident response retainer in place before an incident occurs, rather than engaging for the first time under pressure.
